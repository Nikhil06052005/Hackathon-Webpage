Secrets of the Demon Slayers

What breathing technique should I know?
All participants should have basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Advanced frameworks like React, Vue, or Angular are recommended but not required. Just like Tanjiro, your determination matters more than experience!
How many slayers can form a squad?
Teams can consist of 1-4 members. Solo slayers are welcome, but remember that teamwork makes dream work!
What rewards await the victorious?
The top teams will receive exclusive Demon Slayer merchandise, cash prizes, and opportunities to join elite tech companies. The ultimate champion will be awarded the prestigious Nichirin Blade Trophy!
How will demons (bugs) be judged?
Projects will be evaluated based on creativity, technical difficulty, user experience, and adherence to the theme. Our panel of Hashira (industry experts) will provide fair judgment.
